About Me

I am currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science at NYU Abu Dhabi, where my academic journey intersects with my professional role as a Software Engineer. My expertise spans both frontend and backend development for web applications, with a focus on utilizing technologies like ReactJS for the frontend and Node.js for the backend. Beyond my academic and professional pursuits, I am deeply passionate about delving into the realms of Machine Learning. My ongoing exploration involves a keen interest in understanding various ML models, and I've actively contributed to projects, including those centered around Logistic Regression.
In my leisure time, I indulge in strategic thinking through chess, engaging in both online competitions and friendly matches with friends. Tackling algorithmic challenges on Leetcode has become a stimulating hobby, allowing me to continuously refine my problem-solving skills. Beyond the realm of technology, I find joy in diverse interests, including watching anime and movies. Among my favorite anime episodes are those from "Tokyo Ghoul" and "Hunter x Hunter," where intricate storytelling and compelling characters have left a lasting impact on my appreciation for the medium. These diverse interests collectively shape my holistic approach to both work and leisure, fostering a dynamic and enriching lifestyle.
